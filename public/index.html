Amber Fort

Majestic hill fort known for its stunning Rajput architecture, intricate mirror work, and famous elephant rides. A UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Scenic hilltop fort offering breathtaking panoramic views of Jaipur city, especially stunning during sunset and at night.

The website demonstrates the implementation of Smart City Mission objectives including improved infrastructure, digital services, sustainable development, and citizen-centric governance, all while maintaining the essence of the Pink City.
